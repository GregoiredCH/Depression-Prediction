Depression Form

Information

This form uses Machine Learning to estimate your level of depression. It is based on the NHANES Dataset, a large American public health database used in our depression prediction ML project.

Simply answer the questions below and get your results after you send in your answers.

This questionnaire does not replace the advice of a healthcare professional.
If you think you may be suffering from depression, we strongly encourage you to consult a doctor or specialist.
